About Us

CODE 4 PWD is a self-contained product designed to address the lack of accessible coding and design education for individuals with hearing and speech impairments. It is not part of an existing product or a replacement for another system but is an innovative solution tailored to meet the unique needs of its target users.

Curriculum Overview

This curriculum is accessible and empowers persons with disabilities (PWD) by integrating assistive technologies, tailored teaching methods, and a focus on universal design principles. By doing so, CODE 4 PWD ensures that all learners, regardless of ability, can engage with and benefit from the rich cultural, ethical, and sustainability-focused content, fostering equity and inclusion in education.
